    CV Writting

    Purpose:

    CV showcases your qualifications for jobs.

    Key Sections:

    Personal info, summary, work experience, education, skills, achievements.

    Tailoring:

    Customize for each job using keywords from the job description.

    Format:

    Clear, consistent, error-free, with bullet points and white space.

    Avoid:

    Irrelevant info, typos, inconsistent formatting.

    Goal:

    Stand out to employers and pass automated screening (ATS).

    Job Interviews

    Goal:

    Master verbal/non-verbal communication (93% is non-verbal: tone + body language).

    Prep:

    Research company/job, practice answers, dress professionally.

    Do’s:

    Smile, eye contact, confident posture.

    Use specifics/examples, industry terms.

    Ask questions; listen actively.

    Avoid:

    Vague answers, negativity, no eye contact, short replies.

    Structure Answers:

    "STAR" method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).

    Negotiation Skills

    Goal:

    Reach win-win agreements using BATNA (best alternative) and ZOPA (zone of possible agreement).

    Key Steps:

    Prepare: Research, set goals, know your limits.

    Listen Actively: Understand needs, ask questions.

    Communicate Clearly: Use facts, stay calm, adapt style.

    Tips:

    Build rapport & trust.

    Offer creative solutions.

    Watch non-verbal cues.

    Be ready to walk away if needed.
